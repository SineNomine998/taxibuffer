How to use Taxibuffer
Step 1: Enter your license plate number and taxi license number to authenticate.
Step 2: Select a pickup location from the available queues.
Step 3: Wait for your turn in the queue. You'll receive notifications when it's time to proceed to the pickup zone.
Need help? Contact your TTO for support with registration and access.
